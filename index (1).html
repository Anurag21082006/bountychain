Why BountyChain

Built for Web3 Engineers.
Secured by Code.

Every bounty is a smart contract. Every payout is trustless. Every solution is immutable on-chain.

🔒
Smart Contract Escrow
Bounty funds are locked in an audited ERC-4337 contract the moment a bounty is posted. Payouts trigger automatically on owner verification — no disputes, no delays.
⚡
Automated Payouts
Once a solution is marked verified, the escrow releases funds to the hunter's wallet in the same transaction. Zero intermediaries. Zero wait time. Gas-optimized.
🏆
Role-Based Access
Hunters earn reputation scores. Owners build verified track records. Admins can force-resolve disputes. Every action is signed and logged immutably on-chain.
📎
Rich Media Submissions
Attach code snippets, high-res screenshots, and explainer videos to your bounties and solutions. Media is stored on Cloudinary/IPFS, hashes anchored on-chain.
🎯
Daily Rate Limiting
Spam prevention baked in at both the smart contract and server level. Hunters get 5 submissions per day — enforced on-chain and via JWT-scoped rate limiting middleware.
◈
ENS Identity
Every user is identified by their ENS domain. Reputation, submission history, and earning records are all tied to your non-custodial wallet identity — yours forever.
The Flow

From Problem to Payout
in 4 Steps

01
Post Bounty
Owner describes the technical problem, attaches code/media, and stakes ETH into the smart contract escrow.
02
Hunters Explore
Hunters browse open bounties, filter by tech stack and reward size, and submit solutions with full explanations.
03
Owner Verifies
The bounty owner reviews submissions. On marking one verified, the smart contract automatically pays out the reward.
04
ETH Released
The winning hunter receives the full bounty reward. Their reputation score increases. The bounty is closed on-chain.
